Candidates for the Australian Federal Election 2025

Your choice for this election is a choice that will shape the future for Australia.
Australia needs to have a government which will not abuse their power and will standup for the interests of the people.
I have researched every candidate and have provided references to their policies and statements.
Please ensure you number each and every candidate if you want your vote to be counted.

  • Australian Democrats
      The Australian Democrats uses a science and evidence-based approach to policy.
      This party not only wants to invest in education, skills and training for local workers but they also want to ensure that manufacturing opportunities accentuated will allow repair industries to flourish.
      They place a strong emphasis on a consumers right to repair products as they are concerned about dodgy tactics by corporations who put the repair industry at risk.
      They believe that for too long the repair industry has been in serious decline causing a significant increase in waste, threat to the environment and threat to people's livelihoods if people are increasing relient on parts which are rarely compatible or available in Australia.
      They are determined to implement policies which benefit local industry and local farmers in Australia.
      They believe that Agriculture is the bedrock for food security and therefore wish to review policies to ensure they meet the needs of farmers whilst also ensurinng the benefit of sustainable and self sufficient food production.
      They are concerned that farmers often face unfair contract terms and pricing lacks transparency. Farm machinery is expensive and highly concentrated which limits choice for farmers. Farmers still do not have the right to repair their own machinery as many of the parts are only available overseas.
      In regards to the budget they advocate for a robust, efficient, and effective economy which avoids intergenerational debt and raises revenue effectively.
      To do this, they are looking into insight from various economists and experts which can help pay up the government debt and ensure the economy can work for the best interests of the people.

  • Sustainable Australia
      Sustainable Australia Party is an independent community movement with a science and evidence-based approach to policy.
      This party wants to invest in education and skills training for local workers to secure jobs in factories, farms and small businesses.
      They are placing a strong emphasis on manufacturing is important due to its central role in economic development and resilience and the hugely beneficial flow-on effects to the Australian economy.
      This party wants to better support Australian made products and services through government purchases and public marketing campaigns.
      They will increase taxes on vacant houses and foreign ownership to ensure housing can be affordable for first time buyers.
      They are keen to ensure industries, factories, goods and services can be made in Australia for Australia.
