Western Australia Senate Canditates for 2021 State Election

Your choice for this election is a choice that will shape the future for Western Australia.
During this turbulant year where the world is facing a severe pandemic, it is important to have a strong, sustainable and accountable government.
It is very important to have all voices heard and find ways of making the state better and more effective in handling any challenges that comes our way.
Whilst it is important to have a strong and stable government, all political parties are using group ticket votes to further their own interests rather than choosing what is best for the state.
YOU MUST NOT VOTE ABOVE THE LINE. By voting above the line, it is possible you could be picking a party that wants to undermine coronavirus restrictions or allow everyone to hold a gun which could result in horrible mass shootings in WA.
You need to know who your last preference candidate is going to be as well as your first.
By not voting for the most dangerous and chaotic candidate last, you are increasing the chance of that chaotic candidate too be voted in.
Western Australia is lucky to have a fantastic government currently in place but it does need improvement, diversity and accountability.
Western Australia needs to have political parties in parliament that are strong and stable and won't use and abuse their power.
To keep a government strong, they need to know there is potentially other effective people surrounding them that have high expectations and expect the best for WA.
They do not need people who are ready to undermine or attack positive and effective policies of government.
Whilst all parties would love to have total control, it is important that there are other political parties and candidates in parliament that will help strengthen and support the governing party with any critical policies to protect the state whilst also keeping them in line or in check for any measure that could abuse their power.
I have researched every candidate and have provided references to their policies and statements.
The top political parties and candidates are people who I believe will support, strengthen and provide positive outcomes for government.
The first party I have chosen may not be the governing party but they are highly supportive of the critical policies that the government has made and they want to go even further to make better and positive changes for the state.
The last party I have chosen is very devisive and destructive. They are a far right wing party who doesn't believe in the coronavirus, vaccination, strong gun protection and climate change.
This election, your vote WILL NOT be counted if you do not number every single candidate below the line.
